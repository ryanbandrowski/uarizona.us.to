Site: The Buffet Cocktails
Description: The Buffet Cocktails also known as “The Buff” is Tucson's oldest standing bar opening back in 1934. The bar was originally called Lantern Buffet and has survived many decades without having to move locations or change the majority of its appeal dating back to open, along with the beloved neon sign. It’s had over 15 owners but still manages to be crowned one of the 12 best dive bars in America in 2021 by Esquire magazine. It’s known for the name and the stories that come after from all types of customers in the community, who most likely have their name written in sharpie all over the bar walls.
Statement of Significance: Tucson's oldest bar and an icon in the community.
Physical Description: Brick building with red door, blue accents, and a neon sign.
Location: 538 E 9th St, Tucson, AZ 85705
Owner: Lisha Smith
Style or Cultural Period: Modern
