Site: New Empire Food Market
Description: New Empire Food Market, one of the oldest food markets that has been around since the 1930s, has sat at the exact same locatino for over 50 years. This historical grocery shop is family owned and operated, where it’s seen its fair share of people within the community from low-income families to students and young families. New Empire holds a staple neon sign that can be seen from miles down the road; it's also attached to Empire Laundry with the same history, that is now run by the couple's children. The family has done their absolute best in preserving the history this market has to offer from outside to going back in time as you enter, you’re surrounded by the vintage culture with new food and coffee bar
Statement of Significance: A community staple at the same location for over 50 years.
Physical Description: White building with large green sineage and an iconic neon sign.
Location: 526 E 9th St, Tucson, AZ 85705
Owner: Tun Lim & Anne Lee
