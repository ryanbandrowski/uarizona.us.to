Site: International School of Tucson 
Description: The International School of Tucson is a leading international school committed to offering the Tucson community an unequaled world class international education. Their unique curriculum offers a full language immersion in Spanish, Chinese, French and German in the Preschool and a dual immersion in Spanish, Chinese, French and German in the Junior School. It is a non-profit making, non-selective, co-educational school that provides a comprehensive and well-balanced education to children of wide ranging abilities. 
Statement of Significance: Significant because it offers educational opportunities to the community.
