Site: Drikung Dzogchen Center Of Arizona
Description: The Drikung Dzogchen Center of Arizona was founded in 2014 by Venerable Ontul rinpoche.  Grateful acknowledgement is also made to Cliff Leftwich for the many blessings and donations over the years that have made their shrine room possible, in which their devoted mission has been and will continue to be to bring benefit to all beings.
Statement of Significance: Significant because it provides support for the study and practice of the confluence of Three Rivers of Lineage: Kadampa, Mahamudra, and Dzogchen.
Physical Description: Buddhist temple with a shrine room.
Location: 1038 E. Lester St., Tucson, AZ
Owner: Thomas Cox
Style or Cultural Period: Buddhist.
