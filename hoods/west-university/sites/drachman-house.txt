Site: Drachman House
Description: Built in1907, this American Victorian Bungalow is 1 1/2 stories, with a lava rock Tucson Mountain stone foundation, brick load-bearing walls, and beautiful gardens.
Statement of Significance: Significant because it was constructed during the territorial era.
Physical Description: One and one-half stories with a stone foundation, brick load-bearing walls, a red brick surface, self-trim, high gable roof, and one chimney, with little change in good condition.
Location: 307 E University Blvd, Tucson, AZ 85705
Owner: Lillian Ila Tompkins
Style or Cultural Period: American Victorian Bungalow Influence
