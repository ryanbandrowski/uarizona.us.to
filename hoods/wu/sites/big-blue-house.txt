Site: Big Blue House
Description: Built for John Gardiner in 1900, is under restoration and was a bed and breakfast for many years.
Statement of Significance: Significant because it was constructed during the territorial era. Architecturally significant. Built for John W. Gardner.
Physical Description: One and one-half stories with brick load-bearing walls, a white stucco surface, red trim, wood siding, a low gable roof of red asphalt shingles, two chimneys, and is in fair condition with major alterations.
Location: 144 E University Blvd, Tucson, AZ 85705
Owner: Thomas J. & Anne M. Montgomery
Style or Cultural Period: Queen Anne
