Site: Time Market
Description: Built in 1924, it has continually been a market serving the needs of residents and in addition to some of the best pizza in town, offers excellent espresso drinks, breakfast, gourmet foods, and beer and wine. It has a modern mission front and brick exterior, with post and beam construction. Moving west!
Statement of Significance: Contributing to the character of the historic district.
Physical Description: One story with a post and beam construction, brick walls, a white painted brick, black trim, a flat roof with parapet, no chimneys in good condition, and recently rehabilitated.
Location: 444 E University Blvd, Tucson, AZ 85705
Owner: James & Ann Di Martino & Victor De Blasio
Style or Cultural Period: Modern with Mission Front
